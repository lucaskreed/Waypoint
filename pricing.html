Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?
Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and ACH bank transfers for annual plans.
Is there a discount for annual billing?
Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. That's 2 months free on all plans.
What happens if I exceed my client limit?
We'll notify you and you can easily upgrade to the next tier. No clients will be removed.
Do my clients need to pay?
No! Your subscription covers unlimited client access at no additional cost to them.
